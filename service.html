ITOM

A birds eye view of your IT Operations is indispensable. With various components that make up this ecosystem, from the old legacy to the new cutting edge, there are a lot of blind spot, that could render your systems incapacitated. Using the proactive ServiceNow ITOM platform will mean you will be in control of the health, optimization and have a complete birds eye view of your services, with the power of drilling down on any component. BinMile’s methodology can prevent outages by proactively identifying changes so that you are aware of if there are going to be any adverse effects on your infrastructure. It also aims to reduce event noise, offer insight in to costs and also keep your business policy complaint enterprise wide. A customized ITOM solution, can help you gain clarity into essential business services,infrastructure,and also help improve the health of the services, and introduce automation that will make your systems even more efficient.
